Privacy Policy for Shipment Tracking Chrome Extension

Last Updated: May 28, 2026

1. Purpose of the Extension

The Extension is designed to retrieve shipment tracking information from shipping line, carrier, terminal, and logistics websites for bookings created and managed by the connected hosted application.

The connected hosted application is the sole owner and user of the shipment-related data processed by the Extension.

2. Information Processed

The Extension does not collect personal information from users directly.

The Extension processes shipment-related information including:

  • Container numbers
  • Bill of Lading (BL) numbers
  • Booking numbers
  • Shipment reference numbers
  • Vessel and voyage details
  • Shipment status and tracking events
  • Logistics and delivery updates

3. Use of Information

The information retrieved by the Extension is used solely for operational shipment tracking and logistics management purposes within the connected hosted application.

The Extension does not use any information for:

  • Advertising
  • Marketing
  • Analytics
  • Profiling
  • Sale to third parties

4. Data Ownership

All shipment-related information processed through the Extension belongs exclusively to the connected hosted application and its authorized users.

5. Data Storage

The Extension does not permanently store personal data. Any temporary processing or local caching is limited to operational requirements necessary for the functioning of the Extension and hosted application.

6. Credentials and Authentication

The Extension may interact with websites where users authenticate using their own accounts or browser sessions.

User credentials are not collected, sold, or shared by the Extension. Authentication information remains under the control of the user and browser environment.

7. Third-Party Websites

The Extension interacts with third-party shipping line, carrier, terminal, and logistics websites to retrieve shipment tracking information. These third-party websites operate independently and maintain their own privacy policies and terms of use.

8. Data Sharing

The Extension does not sell, rent, or share information with unrelated third parties.

Shipment tracking information is transmitted only to the authorized hosted application associated with the shipment booking and tracking process.

9. Browser Permissions

The Extension may request permissions necessary to access carrier and logistics websites required for shipment tracking functionality. Such permissions are used only for operational purposes directly related to shipment tracking and logistics processing.

10. Security

Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect processed information and limit access only to required operational functionality.

11. Compliance

The Extension is intended for use only by authorized users of the connected hosted application and for legitimate shipment tracking and logistics operations.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with the revised effective date.
